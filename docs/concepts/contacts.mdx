## What is a Contact?

A contact in Notifuse is a comprehensive profile that includes:

- **Basic Information**: Email address, name, and contact details
- **Custom Fields**: Unlimited additional data points specific to your business
- **List Memberships**: Which lists the contact belongs to and their subscription status
- **Message History**: All messages sent to the contact
