## What is a Broadcast Campaign?

A broadcast campaign in Notifuse is a coordinated email marketing effort that includes:

- **Target Audience**: Specific contact lists or segments
- **Email Content**: Templates with personalized messaging
- **Scheduling**: When and how often emails are sent
- **Tracking**: Performance metrics and engagement analytics
- **A/B Testing**: Optimization through content variations
