## What is a Template?

A template in Notifuse is a pre-designed email layout that includes:

- **Visual Structure**: Header, body, footer, and content sections
- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-optimized layouts that work across devices
- **Dynamic Content**: Liquid variables for personalization
- **Brand Elements**: Colors, fonts, logos, and styling
- **Reusable Blocks**: Modular components for easy customization
